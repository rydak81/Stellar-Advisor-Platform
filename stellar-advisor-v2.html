Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?
Yes, 100%. The strategy call is completely free with no obligation. I'm not going to pitch you anything or pressure you to buy. My goal is to help you get clarity on your Amazon strategy.
What happens on the call?
We'll spend 30 minutes discussing your brand, your challenges, and your goals. I'll give you honest feedback on what I'd prioritize and whether you need outside help at all. You'll leave with clear action items.
Why are you doing this for free?
Two reasons: First, I genuinely enjoy helping brands navigate the confusing Amazon landscape. Second, if you do need a partner down the road, I can make introductions — but only if it's the right fit for you.
Do I need to be a certain size?
I typically work with brands doing $25K-$5M/month on Amazon, but I'm happy to chat with anyone serious about growing their Amazon presence. If I'm not the right fit, I'll point you in the right direction.
How do you make money?
If you decide you need a partner and I make an introduction that leads to a successful partnership, I receive a referral fee from the agency. But that never influences my recommendations—I only suggest partners I genuinely believe are a good fit.
